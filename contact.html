Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard (5-7 days) and express (2-3 days) shipping across Uganda. International shipping is available to most countries worldwide.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN, Airtel), bank transfers, Visa, Mastercard, and PayPal for international customers.

Can I return or exchange items?

Yes! We offer a 14-day return policy for unused items in original condition. Exchange requests are processed within 2-3 business days.

Are all products authentic?

Absolutely! Every item is verified for authenticity and sourced directly from African artisans. We provide certificates of authenticity upon request.
