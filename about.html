About AfriCrafts

Our Story, Mission & Vision

Our Story

AfriCrafts was founded in 2024 with a simple yet powerful mission: to connect the world with the rich heritage of African craftsmanship. What started as a small collection of handwoven baskets from local artisans in Kampala has grown into a thriving e-commerce platform showcasing authentic African fashion and crafts from across the continent.

We recognized that talented African artisans often struggle to reach global markets, while international customers yearn for genuine, ethically-sourced African products. AfriCrafts bridges this gap, creating a marketplace where tradition meets modern commerce, and where every purchase tells a story of cultural pride and economic empowerment.

🎯

Our Mission

To preserve and promote African cultural heritage through authentic crafts and fashion, while empowering local artisans with sustainable income opportunities and global market access.

🌟

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading e-commerce platform for authentic handcrafted goods, recognized globally for quality, authenticity, and positive social impact on artisan communities.

What We Do

01

Curate Authentic Products

We carefully select each item in our collection, ensuring it meets our strict standards for authenticity, quality, and cultural significance. Every product is verified to be genuinely handcrafted using traditional methods.

02

Support Artisan Communities

We work directly with craftspeople, cooperatives, and small businesses across Uganda, Kenya, Ghana, Nigeria, and other African nations. Fair prices ensure artisans earn sustainable incomes while preserving their craft traditions.

03

Deliver Worldwide

From our headquarters in Kampala, we ship authentic African fashion and crafts to customers across East Africa and around the world, making genuine African artistry accessible to everyone.

04

Educate & Inspire

Beyond selling products, we share the stories behind each piece, educating customers about African cultures, traditional techniques, and the artisans who bring these treasures to life.
